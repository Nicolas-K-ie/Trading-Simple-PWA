6 - Your Loss You Will Cut

In Trading, there is a saying that often comes up: "you have to cut your losses and let your gains run". Indeed, as rule number 6 indicates, the miracle method does not exist because each trading plan or signal must have a level of invalidation. The market will not give you 100% winning signals, and the best traders will be able to question themselves and review their trading plan. By cutting your losses, you will no longer have a negative psychological effect on your trading. You should never average your losses! Never! Because every day you will have a loss that you watch everyday increase until the early morning when you may wake up to cut it all off before your capital is destroyed. And maybe you'll never cut until your broker does it for you because of a margin call. On the other hand, cutting your loss allows you to question your method and make it evolve without abandoning it (rule number 4). It is also an additional place in your trading account that you can allocate to a better opportunity. Therefore, it is necessary to know how to stop the bleeding and refocus rather than hoping that your loss will come back in prof it one day. On the other hand, letting your gains run their course allows you, even if you have already reduced the position, to take full advantage of a scenario that you have identif ied upstream and therefore not regret having cut the position too soon. Generally speaking, it is precisely those winning positions that are allowed to run that are the most profitable.
