The Miracle Method You Won't Look For

Many losing traders "take refuge" behind market movements such as "they went looking for my stop". Don't take your losses as a conspiracy theory or personal failure. Trading involves making losses. In fact, it is because you have the rigour that you close trades at a loss. Don't try to be right and get 100% winning positions, but plan to invalidate your trade, signal, trading system, or method. The market will not always go on your way, and there is no such thing as a miracle method. Consider a loss as an integral part of Trading, just as a professional golfer who misses a putt 1 meter f rom the hole is a missed shot on an 18-hole game.
