2 - The Ego You Will Forget

After several losses in a row, a novice trader will behave as if he continues to trade with the same size on these positions. Many will even go so far as to increase the size of their position to "rebuild". The objective is then to recover the previous loss quickly. Except that if the next trade doesn't work, then it will be a vicious circle by increasing the leverage (rule number 1). On the contrary, by reducing the position's size in a period of loss, you will regain conf idence in your Trading, your method and your analysis. You won't be stuck in the dark about taking the next signal your method gives you. After a period of losses, one of the solutions is to take a break so that your ego doesn't get the upper hand.
